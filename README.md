# A Field Guide to Being Human

> The goal isn't to learn 200 mental models. The goal is to become someone with better judgment.

**[humandynamics.guide](https://humandynamics.guide)** — Stories from history, epics, and real life to navigate workplace dynamics, relationships, and hard decisions.

## What is this?

A personal operating system for navigating difficult situations — organized by **situation**, not by book or concept. Every chapter answers one question: "I'm facing X — what do I do?"

Each chapter has:
- **A specific story** from mythology, biography, or history
- **The psychology** behind why it happens (named biases, experiments)
- **A practical move** — what to do this week
- **An interactive quiz** testing real understanding
- **A hand-drawn illustration** and optional audio podcast

## Experience

- 🗺️ **Explore the Map** — Pick your problem. Read the story. Solve the challenge.
- 🎮 **Play the Game** — Run. Hit obstacles. Lessons find you.
- 📱 **Add to Homescreen** — Works offline as a PWA.

## Structure

```
10 Worlds → Regions → Missions (chapters)

World 1: The Mirror (Understanding Yourself)
  └── Identity, Ego, Motivation, Emotions

World 2: The Crowd (Understanding Others)
  └── Incentives, Trust, Status, Difficult People

... and 8 more worlds covering Teams, Leadership,
    Organizations, Decisions, Communication,
    Conflict, Ethics, and Career.
```

## Tech

- Kokoro TTS (podcast audio generation)
