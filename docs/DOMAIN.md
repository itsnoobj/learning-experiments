---

## Selection Criteria

Must be:

- Simple to pronounce
- Simple to search (no spelling ambiguity)
- Simple to remember
- Clear when spoken aloud (no "is that one word or two?" confusion)
- Not boxed into one topic (covers work, relationships, self, ethics, life)
- Related to: humans, stories, learning, patterns, dynamics

## Logo Direction

### Chosen concept: HD Monogram

Two human figures forming the H, with a D curve representing the "dynamic" force between them.

### Devil's Advocate Feedback

- Literal human figures risk "bathroom sign" / HR clip art association
- At 16×16 favicon, figures merge into blobs
- "HD" = "High Definition" is the first association for most people
- Curve-around-figure is a 2018-2024 wellness/therapy cliché

### Recommended direction

- Abstract HD monogram (no literal human pictograms)
- Or: wordmark-first ("humandynamics" with gold accent on "dynamics", tiny HD mark as favicon)
- Must work: single color, dark background, 16×16px, redrawable from memory

### Color Palette

- Charcoal: `#2C2C2C`
- Warm Gold: `#B8860B`
- Cream background: `#FAF7F2`
- Gray (secondary text): `#999`
