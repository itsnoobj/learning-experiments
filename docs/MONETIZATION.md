# Monetization Strategy

## Philosophy

- **Content stays open.** Nobody should be blocked from learning because they can't afford it.
- **Experience is premium.** Audio, tracking, AI features cost money to run — fair to charge.
- **Sustainability is not greed.** Token burns, hosting, 30+ hours per chapter. This needs to sustain itself.
- **Trust over gates.** People who can't afford it get full access, no questions asked.

---

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier              | Access                                              | Price                                       |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Open**          | All text stories, basic quizzes, reflection prompts | Free forever                                |
| **Contributor**   | Full access (audio, tracking, AI features)          | Free — contribute 1 story/month             |
| **Supporter**     | Full access + early chapters + name in credits      | ₹199/month or $5/month                      |
| **Can't afford?** | Full access                                         | ₹0 — use code `FREEACCESS`, no proof needed |

---

## What's Free vs Paid

| Always Free               | Paid (from the moment it launches) |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| All text stories/chapters | Audio narration                    |
| Basic quizzes             | Progress tracking & streaks        |
| Reflection prompts        | AI-powered "apply to my situation" |
| Core multi-lens analysis  | Personal journal / notes           |
|                           | Deep-dive extended analysis        |

### The Rule

> **Never put something behind a paywall that was previously free. Only add NEW things behind the paywall.**

---

## Rollout Phases

### Phase 1: Early Access (now → 20 chapters)

- All content free, no paywall enforced
- Pricing page is **visible from day 1** — shows future plans
- Message on site: _"Free during early access. Paid features coming soon."_
- Collect emails: _"Get notified when audio + tracking launches"_
- Goal: validate content quality, build initial audience

### Phase 2: Premium Launch (20-30 chapters ready)

- Text remains free
- Audio, tracking, AI features launch **as paid**
- Early email subscribers get **3 months free** as thank-you
- New visitors see paywall on premium features immediately
- Contribution model opens (1 story = 1 month access)

### Phase 3: Growth

- Content library grows steadily
- Community contributions expand the story pool
- B2B inquiries come organically (HR/L&D teams find it)
- If B2B demand appears: Team tier at ₹499/person/month with dashboards

---

## "Can't Afford" Policy

- A visible link on the pricing page: _"Can't afford this? No problem."_
- Coupon code `FREEACCESS` — auto-grants full access
- No verification, no proof, no form, no shame
- Abuse rate is historically ~5-10%. Acceptable cost for accessibility.

---

## Contribution Model

Contributors get full access in exchange for value:

| Contribution                         | Credit               |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------- |
| Submit a real-world story (approved) | 1 month full access  |
| Peer-review a chapter                | 1 month full access  |
| Translate a chapter                  | 2 months full access |

This builds the content library AND community. Contributors are co-creators, not charity cases.

---

## Why Not Free Forever?

Costs that scale with users:

- AI token burns (quizzes, personalized feedback, "apply to my situation")
- Audio generation per chapter
- Hosting & CDN
- Time: 30-40 hours per chapter (research, writing, quiz design, multi-lens analysis)

The message on the site:

> _Every chapter takes 30+ hours to research, write, and design. AI costs, hosting, and audio production add up. If you can support this, you keep it free for someone who can't._

---

## B2B (Parked for Later)

Not actively pursuing. If content gets strong enough, HR/L&D teams will find it and reach out. At that point:

- Team tier: ₹499/person/month
- Includes: team dashboards, workshop materials, progress reports
- 10 companies × 20 people = ₹1 lakh/month (funds everything)

Don't sell B2B. Let B2B come to you.
